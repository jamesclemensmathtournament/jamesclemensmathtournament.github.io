The James Clemens Math Tournament was created to help students of various skill levels compete in math. At its heart, the JCMT is less about winning, more about learning. The JCMT is a place where students can learn and grow, and grow enthusiastic towards math. Although we acknowledge that math is an extremely difficult subject, we encourage everyone to give their best effort in solving problems. In addition, in an attempt to help students learn, we have created a tournament that combines games and collaboration to foster the next generation of math enthusiasts. Thank you all for coming to the second edition of the James Clemens Math Tournament, and we hope we will see you all next year. In the meantime, this website can be used to learn more about the JCMT, and to view past tests and answer keys.
